Kasuki Discord Bot - Privacy Policy

Welcome to Kasuki! This policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose information when you use our Discord bot.

Information We Collect

Kasuki collects a limited amount of information to provide you with a better user experience and improve the bot:

  • **Discord User ID:** This unique identifier helps us recognize you when you interact with the bot.
  • **Profile Picture (Optional):** With your permission, Kasuki may access your profile picture to personalize your interactions (e.g., displaying it in a welcome message).
  • **Command Usage:** Kasuki collects data on which commands you use. This helps us understand user preferences and improve the bot's functionality and see if some abuse what is provided.

How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used for the following purposes:

  • **Providing Service:** We use your Discord ID to identify you when you use Kasuki's features.
  • **Personalization (Optional):** If you allow it, your profile picture can be used to enhance your experience.
  • **Improving Kasuki:** We use anonymized command usage data to understand user preferences and improve the bot's functionality.

Data Storage and Security

We take steps to securely store your information. However, no online service can guarantee complete security. We recommend you practice safe online habits.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy at any time. We will notify you of any significant changes. Continued use of Kasuki after such changes signifies your acceptance of the revised policy.
